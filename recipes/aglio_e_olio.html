Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Basic but delicious Spaghetti recipe! A classic of italian cooking

You don't need much for this recipe. It's simplicity is what makes it good

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and add spaghetti
  2. Combine oil and garlic in cold skillet
  3. Stir red pepper flakes, salt, pepper into pasta
  4. Pour in hot garlic oil, add parsley and half the cheese
  5. Toss until combined
  6. Serve with remaining cheese
