Sourdough Sandwich bread

Description

This recipe makes a very simple sourdough sandwich bread. It assumes you already have an active sourdough starter

It makes for a very versatile and delicious bread!

Ingredients

  • 100 g (about 1/2 cup) active starter, see notes above
  • 430 g water, (or less, see notes above) room temperature
  • 512 g (4 cups) bread flour, such as King Arthur Flour
  • a few tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • room temperature butter, for greasing

Steps

  1. Mix the dough
  2. Perform several folds
  3. Bulk ferment
  4. Shape and place into pan
  5. Let rise again
  6. Bake
  7. Cool, slice and enjoy
